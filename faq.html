Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about therapy at Mental Wellness Montclair.

Therapy can benefit anyone who is feeling stuck, overwhelmed, or simply seeking personal growth. If you're experiencing persistent sadness, anxiety, relationship difficulties, or life transitions that feel hard to navigate alone, therapy can help. We offer a free 15-minute consultation to help you decide if it's the right fit with no obligation.

Standard therapy sessions are 45–50 minutes. Most clients begin with weekly sessions, which is the standard frequency for building momentum and establishing therapeutic rapport. As you progress, you may transition to bi-weekly or monthly maintenance sessions. Your therapist will work with you to determine the best schedule for your needs and goals.

Yes, we accept Aetna, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Cigna, United Healthcare, Horizon BCBS, Medicare, Oxford, and Optum. To verify your specific plan's coverage, you can call us at (973) 210-7310 or fill out our contact form. We'll check your benefits and explain your coverage, including any copays or deductibles, before your first session.

Both options are equally effective for most mental health concerns. In-person sessions offer a dedicated, distraction-free space outside your home and allow for a different kind of connection. Teletherapy offers flexibility and convenience — you can attend from anywhere in New Jersey using your computer, tablet, or phone. Many clients use a combination of both. We'll help you decide what works best for your lifestyle and preferences.

Getting started is simple. Call us at (973) 210-7310, email admin@mentalwellnessmontclair.com, or fill out our contact form. We'll schedule a free 15-minute phone consultation to discuss your needs, answer your questions, and match you with the right therapist. From there, you'll book your first full session and begin your journey.

Your first session, often called an intake session, is a chance for you and your therapist to get to know each other. You'll discuss what brought you to therapy, your personal history, current challenges, and goals. There's no pressure to share everything at once — you can go at your own pace. Your therapist will explain how they work, answer your questions, and begin developing a treatment plan tailored to you.

Our self-pay rates range from $150–$250 per session depending on the clinician and type of service. We offer a limited number of sliding-scale slots for clients with financial need. We also provide superbills that you can submit to your insurance company for potential out-of-network reimbursement. Contact us for details.

Yes, we have a board-certified psychiatrist on staff, Dr. James Okonkwo, who provides comprehensive psychiatric evaluations and medication management. We strongly believe in an integrated approach — your medication provider works closely with your therapist to ensure coordinated, holistic care.

Yes, we provide therapy for adolescents ages 12 and up. Our clinicians who specialize in adolescent mental health use developmentally appropriate approaches, including DBT-informed therapy, family-based interventions, and creative modalities. We also offer parent coaching and family sessions as part of our adolescent treatment.

We understand that life happens. We ask for at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations (less than 24 hours) and no-shows may be charged the full session fee. You can cancel or reschedule by calling or emailing us — no questions asked. We're happy to help you find an alternative time that works.
