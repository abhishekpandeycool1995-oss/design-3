Our Story

A Practice Built on Compassion

Mental Wellness Montclair was founded with a simple belief: everyone deserves access to exceptional mental health care. What started as a small private practice has grown into a thriving community of therapists dedicated to helping individuals, couples, and families heal and grow.

Our founders, Dr. Sarah Chen and Michael Torres, LCSW, shared a vision of creating a space where clinical excellence meets genuine human connection. They brought together a team of like-minded clinicians who believe in the transformative power of therapy.

Today, we serve over 500 clients annually from our beautiful Montclair office and through our virtual platform. Our team continues to grow, but our commitment to personalized, compassionate care remains at the heart of everything we do.
