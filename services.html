Our Services

Comprehensive mental health care designed for every stage of your journey.

🧠

Individual Therapy

One-on-one sessions with a licensed therapist addressing anxiety, depression, trauma, grief, life transitions, and personal growth. Using CBT, DBT, psychodynamic, and mindfulness approaches tailored to your needs.

Who it's for: Adults and adolescents (16+) seeking personal support and growth.

What to expect: A safe, confidential space to explore your thoughts and feelings with evidence-based guidance.

Book Now →
💑

Couples Therapy

Strengthen your relationship with proven approaches including Gottman Method and Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT). Improve communication, rebuild trust, and deepen your emotional connection.

Who it's for: Couples at any stage, whether navigating conflict or seeking enrichment.

What to expect: Structured sessions with both partners, focused exercises, and actionable tools.

Book Now →
👨‍👩‍👧

Family Therapy

Improve family dynamics, resolve conflicts, and build healthier communication patterns. We work with families of all configurations to create lasting positive change.

Who it's for: Families navigating conflict, transitions, or seeking stronger bonds.

What to expect: Collaborative sessions with family members, skill-building, and facilitated dialogue.

Book Now →
👥

Group Therapy

Connect with others in a supportive, facilitated group setting. Groups available for anxiety, grief, social skills, parenting, and LGBTQ+ support.

Who it's for: Individuals seeking peer support and shared healing experiences.

What to expect: Weekly 90-minute sessions with 6–10 participants and one facilitator.

Book Now →
🧘

Teletherapy

Access therapy from anywhere in New Jersey through our secure, HIPAA-compliant video platform. Same quality care with greater flexibility and convenience.

Who it's for: Busy professionals, parents, and anyone preferring remote sessions.

What to expect: Simple setup, secure connection, and the same therapeutic experience as in-person.

Book Now →
⭐

Psychiatric Services

Comprehensive psychiatric evaluations, medication management, and integrated care coordinated with your therapist for a holistic approach to mental health.

Who it's for: Individuals who may benefit from medication alongside therapy.

What to expect: Thorough evaluation, collaborative treatment planning, and ongoing monitoring.
